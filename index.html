Welcome to the website for the West Wales Linux User Group!

We are a friendly bunch of Linux enthusiasts who are based in the West Wales area.

We aim to provide free and helpful support to local users of the Linux operating system, as well as help people learn about Linux and Free and Open Source Software in general.

We welcome anyone who's interested in Linux, regardless of experience.

To find out more about us, look at the About page.
